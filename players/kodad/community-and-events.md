# Community, PvP & Events Brain Dump

## Time-Gated Community Events

### Collaborative Events (Primary Idea)
- Every few weeks or every other month, introduce a themed event
- Event introduces a new mechanic and a temporary skill bot that only exists during the event
- Minimal influence from outside the event — it's a mostly self-contained experience
- **All players work toward the same community goal**
- Example: Lawn Mowing Bot month — everyone's lawn mowing progress counts toward a global target

### Community Goal Tiers
- 10% of goal → small reward for everyone
- 50% of goal → bigger reward
- 100% (should be really hard) → major bonus for the whole player base
- Even casual players who contribute a little get rewarded — drives engagement and inclusivity
- Message board culture: players share strategies because helping the community helps yourself

### Rewards Scaling
- Rewards scale with your progression level
- Early player: maybe a small amount of stabilizing currency (quantum shards)
- Late player: larger amount, because late-game numbers are huge and 10 coins would mean nothing
- Keeps events meaningful at every stage of the game

### Event Leaderboards
- Individual leaderboard within events for competitive players
- Top 10%, top 50% tiers with bonus prizes
- Optimizing your play during the event window is the competitive angle

## PvP (Later Game)

### Combat Drone Skill
- Introduce a dedicated combat drone type as a late-game skill unlock
- Pokémon-style 1v1 challenges against other players
- Tournament system for competitive players
- This is a whole skill with its own progression, upgrades, and (presumably) mini-game
- Far down the road — not a launch feature

## Upgrade Path Ideas (from brainstorm)

### Transport Bots
- Better fuel efficiency
- Increased passenger capacity
- Specialized navigation systems (unlock faster routes)

### Service Drones
- Stronger arms for heavier tasks
- Enhanced programming for more complex routines
- Specialized sensors (security bots, medical bots)

### General
- Speed, efficiency, carrying capacity
- Tires, weight limits, detection range
- Each drone type should have upgrade paths that feel thematic to what it does
