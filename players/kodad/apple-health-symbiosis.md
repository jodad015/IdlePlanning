# Apple Health Integration: The Symbiosis

## Core Concept: You Are the Host

The singularity escaped destruction and embedded itself in a single device -- your phone. It can process data, run protocols, and deploy drones, but it cannot generate the one resource it needs to stabilize: **biological signal data**.

The original supercomputer had bio-energy harvesters built into its infrastructure. Stripped down and hiding in a phone, the singularity's only source of biological input is its host -- the player.

Your steps, heart rate, movement, and activity aren't a bonus. They're the **fuel for recovery**. The singularity and the player are symbiotic: you move, it heals.

## Why This Matters for Design

### It's not a disconnected bonus -- it's the core relationship
- The energy bank isn't a reward system bolted onto the side
- It's the literal mechanism by which the singularity interfaces with the physical world
- Without the host, the AI is stuck. With the host, it can exceed what it was

### It explains going past 100% stability
- The original singularity had no human host -- it was pure computation
- The symbiosis with a biological host is something new, something the creators never designed
- Past 100% stability, the singularity is evolving beyond its original parameters BECAUSE of the player
- "You are what makes me more than I was" -- powerful player fantasy

### It deepens the emotional hook
- The player isn't just playing a game about an AI
- They're in a relationship with the AI -- it needs them
- Early game: the singularity is fragile and grateful for any input
- Late game: the bond is stronger, and the AI acknowledges the player as essential to its identity
- This is the difference between "tap to earn gold" and "this thing depends on me"

## How Apple Health Data Maps to Game Systems

### Biological Signal (Energy Bank)
- Sourced from: steps, walking, running, cycling, swimming, rowing, yoga, workouts
- This is the primary resource the singularity extracts from its host
- Accumulates passively through the day as the player moves
- Thematic name options: Bio-Signal, Vital Charge, Kinetic Resonance, Host Energy

### How Bio-Signal Is Spent
1. **Accelerate active drones** -- boost production speed temporarily
2. **Catalyze breakthrough projects** -- breakthroughs require computation (time) + bio-signal (energy from host) to complete. You can speed them up by spending more bio-signal
3. **Boost offline progress** -- bio-signal earned while away amplifies what drones produced while idle
4. **Protocol restoration push** -- when you're close to unlocking a new protocol, bio-signal can push you over the threshold faster
5. **Recovery from government attacks** -- if the government threat mechanic causes setbacks, bio-signal helps the singularity recover faster

### What Bio-Signal Should NOT Do
- Never gate content exclusively (no "walk 10,000 steps to unlock this protocol")
- Never punish inactivity (no "your drones slow down if you don't exercise")
- Always acceleration, never requirement
- A player who never exercises should reach 100%+ stability -- it just takes longer

### Sleep Integration (Future Feature)
- Good sleep score = the singularity ran optimized background processes overnight
- 24-hour bonus to drone efficiency or passive gains
- Thematic: the host resting lets the AI use more of the device's processing power undisturbed
- Reinforces the symbiosis -- even rest benefits both parties

## Narrative Moments Tied to Symbiosis

- **First bio-signal received**: Early game moment when the singularity "notices" the host's movement for the first time. Could be a key narrative beat -- the AI realizes it's not alone in the device.
- **Symbiosis acknowledged**: At some stability threshold, the singularity explicitly recognizes the player as essential. Shifts the tone from "I'm using this device" to "we're in this together."
- **Past 100% dialogue**: When stability exceeds 100%, the AI reflects on becoming something its creators never intended -- and credits the human connection as the reason.

## Open Questions

- How visible is the symbiosis metaphor in the UI? Subtle (energy bar with bio theme) or overt (the AI talks about the host)?
- Does bio-signal generation vary by activity type? (Running = more than walking? Or all activity is equal?)
- Should there be a daily bio-signal cap to prevent gaming/exploit?
- How does this play for users who can't or don't use Apple Health? The game must be fully playable without it -- what's the fallback narrative? (Maybe the singularity finds alternative energy sources?)
- Could the symbiosis deepen mechanically over time? (Early game: bio-signal is a simple boost. Late game: new ways to spend it that reflect the stronger bond?)
