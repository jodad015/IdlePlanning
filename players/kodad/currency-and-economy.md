# Currency & Economy Brain Dump

## Currency Structure: Two Tiers

### Skill-Specific Currencies
Each drone type earns its own currencies tied to its theme. These are used to buy upgrades within that skill.

- **Fishing drone**: catches different kinds of fish — higher progression unlocks rarer fish types, which are used for better upgrades within the fishing skill
- **Mining drone**: different gemstone types
- **Transport/logistics**: logistics credits
- **Service drones**: service points
- **Medical/educational**: data packets
- Each skill can have **multiple currency types** that unlock as you progress within the skill
- Early currencies become easier to earn once you have higher-tier upgrades (feels good, shows progress)

### Overarching Currency
A global currency earned in small amounts from all skills, used for big cross-cutting upgrades.

- Theme ideas: **"Core Processing Power"** or **"Quantum Shards"** — something more unique than generic gems
- Earned from all drone types in small quantities
- Used for: big one-time purchases, upgrades that power up everything, cross-skill investments
- Could also be spent on breakthrough projects or big progression gates

## Economy Philosophy

### No Maintenance Drains
- Don't want a system where you're constantly dumping currency just to keep a skill running
- If you're moving slow on a skill because you're not investing energy, it shouldn't also be draining your bank
- Idle Obelisk Minor model is fine: fuel is an **optional boost**, not a requirement — drones operate without it but fueling gives bonuses

### Progress Gates Are OK
- Big currency costs to unlock the next tier/milestone = good
- Constant small drains = bad
- The feel should be: save up → hit a milestone → feel rewarded, not: constantly bleeding resources

### Optional Boost Sinks
- OK to have systems where you dump currency to temporarily boost a skill
- Idle Obelisk Minor reference: sink gems → create fuel → fuel gives bonuses per skill (ore bonus, fishing bonus, stargazing bonus)
- Converting overarching currency into skill-specific boosts is a healthy currency loop

## Monetization Philosophy
- Same as good idle games: spending real money just makes things go faster
- Nothing exclusive behind paywalls
- Free-to-play gets everything eventually
- Apple Health energy works the same way — acceleration only, never gating
