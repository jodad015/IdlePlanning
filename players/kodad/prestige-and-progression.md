# Prestige & Progression Brain Dump

## Prestige Philosophy

### No Full-Game Resets
- Don't want to prestige the entire game and lose access to skills you enjoy
- Getting locked out of a fun skill for a day while you grind back up = bad feel
- Want prestige to be per-skill or per-system, not global

### Breakthrough Projects (Prestige Alternative)
Instead of resetting, you dedicate a portion of a drone's productivity to a "breakthrough project" that permanently enhances the skill.

How it works:
- Hit a major milestone for a bot type
- Choose to divert ~10% of that bot's production for a period
- The bot keeps working (just 10% slower) while the diverted power fills a progress bar
- It's "background research" — not a full stop
- When complete: permanent, powerful upgrade (not just a number boost — something qualitatively different)
- Example: Fishing bot breakthrough → starts catching giant rare fish with huge bonuses
- Example: Landscaping bot breakthrough → creates self-regenerating topiaries that provide special resources

### Energy Bank + Breakthrough Synergy
- You can also spend from your energy bank (Apple Health power) to speed up breakthrough projects
- Creates a strategic choice: run bots at full speed for daily resources, OR invest energy into breakthrough research
- Connects the Apple Health integration directly to the prestige system

## Cross-Skill Progression Gates

### Combo Unlocks
- Some upgrades require multiple drone types at certain levels
- Example: electrician bot at level X + plumbing bot at level Y = new unlock
- Encourages strategic distribution across skills rather than hyper-focusing one drone
- Creates "optimal routes" for progression that the community can theorycraft

### Singularity Stability as Central Metric
- Overall game progression measured by stability percentage
- Stability increases from upgrades, skill unlocks, and milestone completions
- Shareable with community ("I'm 42.7% stable")
- This is the equivalent of Idle Obelisk Minor's obelisk level

## Random Events (Per-Skill)
- Market crashes, tech breakthroughs, etc. that affect skill-specific currencies
- Must be **siloed to individual skills** — a market crash hits the trading bot, not your landscaping
- Adds variety and strategic decision-making within skills
- Day trading bot in particular could have very complex event systems
